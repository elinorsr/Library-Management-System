OVERVIEW

"The Crimson Conquest: A Romance of Pizarro and Peru" by Charles B. Hudson is a historical fiction novel set during the Spanish conquest of Peru. The story follows Cristoval and his experiences during the expedition led by Pizarro, highlighting the challenges, betrayals, and moral dilemmas faced by the Spanish conquistadors. As Cristoval navigates loyalty, courage, and love amidst the turmoil of war and conquest, the novel delves into the complexities of history and human relationships in a land torn apart by conflict.
